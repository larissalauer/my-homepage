01 / Academic Research

Unified Meta-Regression Model for RVAS.

Rare variant association studies (RVAS) of complex traits have emerged as a powerful approach to advance drug discovery and diagnostics. We applied a unified meta regression model to 1,144 continuous phenotypes from UK Biobank using single variant summary statistics obtained from Genebass. For each gene discovery, we make available a characterization of whether constrained sites are associated with the phenotype, whether pathogenic sites determined by structural based predictions are associated with phenotype, and whether broader loss-of-function or missense variant annotation better explains the summary statistics observed.

02 / Project

Education Data Work @ EdTrust.

EdTrust is a non-profit committed to improving educational equity, with a focus on racial equity. As a data consultant at EdTrust, I developed reproducible data workflows, automating the ingestion of data from public sources, data cleaning and transformation. This work was done in partnership with EdTrusts' teams of researchers - who rely on reliable and high-quality data for monitoring and reporting on trends in U.S. education.

Much of the data I worked with is also available to view on EdTrust's College Results site, which makes available educational outcome data (including financial outcomes, financial aid, & graduation/retention rate data) and student body data (such as the diversity of the student body and the selectivity of admissions) for both four-year (public/private) and two-year (public) degree-granting institutions.
